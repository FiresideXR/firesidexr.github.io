FiresideXR is a semi-decentralized social XR platform with a focus on user comfort and live event. FiresideXR's decentralized aspects allow it to persist in the event of total server shutdown.
("decentralized aspects" do not include cryptocurrencies)

FiresideXR is built by Anders Olsen with art from Cooper Ludt. Anders and Cooper met on a now defunct social VR platform in late 2018. Anders is pursing studies in computer science, while Cooper explores graphic design, 3d modeling, and many other forms of digital art. Both are studying game design.

Neither of them felt satisfied with the VR platforms they tried. In 2024, they teamed up to create their perfect social platform. Combining every aspect of social XR that they loved, and building it on a protocol that could outlive them.

We don't want to create a billion dollar startup, we want to make a new home for us and our friends.

We hope you'll join us.
