EVERGREEN

We've named the backend of FiresideXR the "Evergreen Protocol". This is an open protocol that allows for authenticated, moderated social XR without relying on any one server to remain active. Because we don't rely on a single backend server, all our servers can go offline without killing FiresideXR. While not designed with third party clients in mind, these should all work if they adhere to the protocol.

The Evergreen Protocol is in early development and subject to sudden change. Documentation will become available as it stabilizs, though this shouldn't impact most users.
