EVERGREEN

We've named the backend of FiresideXR "Evergreen". Evergreen allows for authenticated, moderated social XR without relying on any single server to remain active. Evergreen is built on top of libp2p, the same technology behind IPFS and many other decentralized projects. Because nothing depends on a central backend, our servers can go offline without killing FiresideXR.

The Evergreen Protocol is in early development and subject to sudden change. Documentation will become available as it stabilizes, though this shouldn't impact most users.
